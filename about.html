Edge Mixing was founded by producer/engineer Anthony Dini in 2010 to help independent artists and bands get top quality sonic representation at a reasonable cost.

Anthony specializes in mixing Pop, Rock, Urban and EDM genres, and has worked successfully with many up-and-coming acts, including Hollie Cavanagh (American Idol), Nick Tangorra (X-Factor, Top 20 Album, iTunes US, 2015), and Osvaldo Supino (#3 album iTunes Italy, 2015).

Anthony's aptitude for mixing, combined with his passion for music and his powerful assortment of high-end gear, form an unbeatable method for capturing that elusive 'major label' sound.
